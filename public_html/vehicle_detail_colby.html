Thorton Colby CX410-B

Vehicle Description

The Thorton Colby CX410-B is a durable off-road capable vehicle made to withstand the roughest of terrains in the Badlands. Built like a tank but dressed like a cruiser, it's a favorite among nomads and survivalists alike.

Specs & Features
  • Top Speed: 140 mph
  • Drive Type: 4WD
  • Reinforced Suspension for rough terrain
  • Nomad grade survival kit preinstalled
  • All-weather performance
