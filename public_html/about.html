About NeonRPM

NeonRPM was built with a passion for futuristic vehicles and storytelling. We bring the thrill of Night City's streets into a visual showcase of the most iconic rides from Cyberpunk 2077.

Meet the Team

Muamar Reyes (El Capitan)

A fixer from Santo Domingo and loyal Night City native, El Capitan connects you with top-tier vehicles and opportunities. Known for his charisma and vast network, he’s the bridge between the underground and the open road.

Keo Jeffrey

Founder of NeonRPM and creative behind the concept. Merges cyberpunk aesthetic with immersive user experience to spotlight the vehicles of tomorrow.

V

A legendary mercenary and street legend in the making. Known for navigating high-risk gigs with precision, V works as a trusted courier for Muamar Reyes, delivering rare and high-end vehicles across Night City with speed, style, and sometimes firepower.

Our Journey

2070 – Ignition Begins

NeonRPM is founded in Santo Domingo as an underground tuner shop by a former netrunner and rogue fixer.

2072 – On the Map

NeonRPM gains legal standing through alliances with mid-tier fixers, becoming corp-neutral and publicly operational.

2073 – Net Launch

Launch of the first interactive web-holo showroom accessible via the Net. Sales spike city-wide.

2075 – Corporate Clash

Arasaka attempts to breach NeonRPM systems. Survived by strong netrunner fortification. Gains street cred.

2076 – Expansion

Second location opens in Pacifica to serve mercs and outlaws. Offers exclusive edgerunner mods.

2077 – Hub of Night City

NeonRPM recognized by Afterlife fixers as the #1 independent auto dealer in Night City.
